
App name: My chord archive

Description: Collect your unique song base!
Of course you know whats wrong with ordinary chords librarys. You can choose from douzens versions of same song, but not one of them not good enough. The first one have a weird formatting. Another one missed the last chorus. The third one have a wrong chords.

Collect your unique song base by youself! Find base template of needed song in web and just add it as a new song. Then you can do with that everything what you want! Edit lyrics, font, size. Change chords, tonality and even colors and backgrounds. Rate your skill for every song and be better day by day with "My chord archive".
